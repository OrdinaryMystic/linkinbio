Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay you?

I accept PayPal, Cash App, and all major credit cards. I’ll send a secure payment link with your confirmation.

How long does a reading take?

As long as it needs to. It may be 30 minutes or 2 hours. Quality and clarity come first.

How soon will I receive a recorded reading?

Usually within 3–5 business days. If timing is sensitive, note that on the form and I’ll do my best to accommodate.

What do I need to provide?

A brief intention or area of focus helps. For astrology, your birth date, time, and place make charts more precise.

What kinds of questions are welcome?

Life direction, relationships, creativity, vocation, and spiritual growth are great fits. I don’t do medical, legal, or lottery predictions.

What’s your rescheduling or cancellation policy?

Please give 24 hours’ notice to reschedule. If something urgent comes up, email me and we’ll find a new time.

What if the reading doesn’t resonate?

If something feels off, reply to the confirmation email within a week. I’ll offer a brief clarifying follow-up.
