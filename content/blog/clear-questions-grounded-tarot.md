Most tarot advice leans hard into symbolism, mystery, and ritual. Ordinary Mystic takes a different approach: clear questions, calm conversation, and practical next steps.

### Start with one real question

Instead of asking *“What does the universe want me to know?”* try something like:

- *“What am I missing about this situation at work?”*
- *“What’s a useful way to look at this relationship right now?”*
- *“What should I be paying attention to over the next few months?”*

These questions are specific enough to be useful and open enough to invite perspective.

### No fate, no doom

Tarot is not a script you are forced to follow. In this practice, cards are a structured way to:

- Notice patterns in how you think and react
- Explore different ways to frame a situation
- Choose actions that feel aligned and realistic

You always keep agency. The cards do not.

### What a grounded reading feels like

A session with Ordinary Mystic is more like a focused, honest conversation than a performance. We map out:

1. Where you are now  
2. What’s pulling at your attention  
3. What you can experiment with next  

No smoke, no spectacle—just clear language and practical insight. If you want tarot that respects your intelligence and your skepticism, you’re in the right place.
