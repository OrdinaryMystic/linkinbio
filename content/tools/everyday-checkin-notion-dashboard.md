The Everyday Check-In Dashboard is built for people who want reflective practice without turning it into a second job.

It gives you one calm page where you can:

- Log your mood, energy, and focus in a few clicks  
- Capture quick notes about what’s actually affecting your day  
- Notice patterns over weeks instead of reacting to one bad afternoon  

No streaks, no gamification, no pressure—just a quiet place to keep track of how you’re really doing.
