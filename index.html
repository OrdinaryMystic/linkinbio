Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Logistics

How quickly will I hear back after booking?

I'll respond within 1 business day with next steps. For recorded readings, expect delivery in 5–7 business days. For live readings, we'll schedule within 1–2 business days.

How are recordings delivered?

Via private YouTube link sent directly to your email.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Life happens! Please give 24 hours notice if possible. Cancellations made with at least 24 hours notice are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of your scheduled reading are non-refundable, but we can work together to reschedule when possible.

Types of Readings & What to Expect

What readings do you offer?

Full Birth Chart Reading (90 minutes): Deep dive into your natal chart plus timing techniques to understand your life story and what's coming

Mini Birth Chart Reading (25 minutes): Focused exploration of a single topic in your natal chart

Birthday Reading (90 minutes): Solar return analysis, annual profections, and year-ahead forecast

What information do you need from me?

Your birth date, exact birth time (as precise as possible), and birth location. When you fill out the booking form, let me know how certain you are about your birth time—that helps me calibrate the reading.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

I'm probably not the best fit for you. My approach relies heavily on precise birth times for accurate house placements and timing work. I don't offer chart rectification services. If you're serious about getting a reading but don't have your birth time, I'd encourage you to work with a rectification specialist first, then bring that chart to me. I won't turn you away if you book anyway, but just know the reading will be significantly less detailed.

Can I ask specific questions?

Absolutely—and I encourage it! General readings produce general results. The whole point is to help you understand your life and move forward with clarity. Specific questions = specific insights.

Preparing for Your Reading

How should I prepare?

Come with an open mind. Think back over at least the past 1–2 years—I may ask about dates and major events. Consider bringing:

• A calendar or your phone's camera roll to jog your memory about life events and their timing
• Specific questions you want to explore

Live vs. Recorded—what's the difference?

Live readings are collaborative. I'll ask questions, you'll provide feedback, and we'll dig into what's most relevant together in real time (via Zoom).

Recorded readings are essentially blind readings with no input from you during the process. You'll receive a video walkthrough of your chart. If you're not comfortable with back-and-forth dialogue, this is your best option.

Either way, bring a willingness to hear potentially hard truths about yourself—that's where the growth happens.

My Approach & Philosophy

How would you describe your reading style?

Think of it like a weather report for your life. I look at how cosmic energies have impacted you in the past, and from that we can extrapolate how those same energies are likely to show up in the future. It's all about potentials, not rigid predictions. My job is to help you prepare internally for the energies happening externally—so you can work with them instead of against them.

I use a traditional Hellenistic framework, which means events and timing matter. But the focus is always empowering: understanding the terrain so you can navigate it wisely.

What topics are off-limits?

I don't read on health issues or "when will I die" questions. I'm happy to discuss relationships (including divorce), but astrology doesn't give black-and-white answers like "this IS divorce" or "this is NOT divorce." Instead, we look at challenges and ease within relationships—what you do with that information is up to you.

Is astrology compatible with Christianity?

Yes. God created the planets; Genesis tells us the sun and moon were made to mark time and festivals. Astrology doesn't involve communicating with the dead or spirits—it's about reading the energies of creation itself.

That said, I'm a Christian who practices astrology, not a "Christian astrologer." I won't bring my faith into your reading unless you're a Christian and that's important to you. For me personally, I never trust astrology over prayer or the Holy Spirit's guidance—but I've found astrology is one way to tune into the energies of the universe God created. Surprisingly (or not), those energies often align beautifully with what I discern through prayer.

What if I'm not religious?

My approach resonates regardless of your faith tradition (or lack thereof). I use traditional Hellenistic techniques that aren't tied to any one belief system. You don't need to share my worldview to get value from the reading.

What about fate vs. free will?

Free will is everything. Nothing is predetermined. Yes, cosmic energies can be strong and difficult to resist—they have a "fate-like" quality—but you always have agency. You have the power to shape your reality. Astrology just helps you see the currents so you can swim with them or against them intentionally.

Techniques & Training

What astrological techniques do you use?

I work primarily from a Hellenistic/traditional framework with some modern insights:

• Whole sign houses only (this is non-negotiable for me—I won't read your chart in Placidus unless you really insist, and even then it'll be an aside)
• Traditional rulership (Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto don't rule signs in my system; they move too slowly to significantly impact individual charts, and traditional rulership just works better)
• Timing techniques (in order of priority):
  - Annual profections
  - Transits
  - Solar returns
  - Progressed charts (rarely—only if specifically needed)
  - Zodiacal releasing (I'm still building confidence here, so I only incorporate it if you specifically request it)

Tropical or sidereal?

Always tropical. I know nothing about sidereal astrology. The system of astrology and the meanings of the signs are based on the seasons of the earth, and tropical just makes sense to me. If sidereal works for you, that's great—but I'm not a sidereal astrologer.

Do you work with fixed stars or calculated points?

No. I don't look at fixed stars, asteroids, or most calculated points (except the lunar nodes, and the Arabian Lots if we're doing zodiacal releasing). There's enough to talk about for days just looking at the 10 main planets. I'd rather go deep on what matters most than spread the reading thin across every possible data point.

How long have you been studying astrology?

Two years. I know that might sound short, but here's the thing: I have ADHD hyperfixations, which means I've dedicated basically all my free time to learning astrology. I've read voraciously, taken courses, and now I've hit a wall—I'm not going to learn anything else from books. The only way forward is to read as many charts as possible.

I'm not claiming to be an expert (hence my affordable pricing). But I have good reviews, and I genuinely believe you'll get a solid, insightful reading. Plus, you're booking me now while I'm still cheap—before I become a world-renowned astrology expert charging hundreds per hour. 😉

What got you into astrology?

The classic story: breakup, new hobby. I got divorced in 2023, stumbled across a TikTok about divorce and astrology that resonated deeply, booked a mini reading on a TikTok live, and was instantly hooked. Astrology helped me process my divorce and heal in ways I didn't expect. That experience taught me how powerful these symbolic systems can be for self-reflection—and I fell in love with the practice.

How do you see astrology?

I see astrology as external scaffolding for self-reflection. Humans are terrible at looking at ourselves honestly—our brains have defense mechanisms and blind spots everywhere. But when we throw our patterns into the sky and look at them through symbols? Suddenly we get curious instead of defensive. There are no blind spots in astrology. It invites us to explore who we are, who we're becoming, and how we're growing—without the usual ego interference. That's the magic.
