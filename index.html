Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Logistics

How quickly will I hear back after booking?

I'll respond within 1 business day with next steps. For recorded readings, expect delivery in 5–7 business days via a private YouTube link. For live readings, we'll get you on the schedule within 1–2 business days.

What is the difference between Live and Recorded readings?

Live readings (Zoom, In-Person, or TikTok): These are collaborative. We talk through the cards in real-time, allowing for follow-up questions and immediate feedback.

Recorded readings: These are "blind" readings. I recorded a video walkthrough of your cards (or chart) and send it to you. This is a great "sit back and listen" option if you aren't ready for a 1-on-1 dialogue.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Life happens! Please give 24 hours' notice. Cancellations with 24 hours' notice get a full refund. Within 24 hours, readings are non-refundable, but I'm happy to work with you to reschedule.

Tarot Readings

What is your Tarot "style"?

I am an "Ordinary Mystic." That means I'm here to demystify the cards. I don't use incantations or "magic" rituals. I use the Rider-Waite-Smith deck (exclusively) as a psychological tool—a piece of external scaffolding to help you see past your own ego and blind spots. It's about getting a fresh perspective on the past, present, and future.

Do I need to choose a specific spread?

No. I don't offer a menu of spreads because I tailor the layout to your specific situation. That said, I am a huge proponent of the Celtic Cross. My training comes from Dusty White (Academy of Divination), so expect in-depth, structured readings that actually answer your questions rather than giving vague "vibes."

Why are your TikTok Live readings free right now?

I'm focused on building a community. For my first 100 TikTok Live readings, the service is free. If you're willing to have your reading done publicly to help me grow the platform, I want to honor that by giving my time. After the first 100, the price will move to $15.

Note: Even for free readings, you must fill out the booking form so I can track my count and send you a written summary of our session.

Can I ask about other people (exes, bosses, etc.)?

I don't mind reading on "third parties," but we will always frame it through your perspective. It is difficult to connect with the energy of someone who isn't there and hasn't consented, so I manage expectations: we'll look at how you can navigate the relationship or what the cards suggest about the dynamic from where you are standing.

Astrology & Combined Sessions

Do you still offer Astrology?

Yes! While I am leading with Tarot, I still offer Hellenistic Astrology services. You can book a standalone Astrology session or a Tarot & Astrology Combo.

Note: All sessions are now capped at a maximum of 60 minutes.

Why do you ask for my birth time if I'm only booking Tarot?

I use the Whole Sign House system for astrology. Even for a Tarot reading, having your birth info allows me to ground myself in your "cosmic weather" before we start. However, unless you book a "Combo" session, I won't spend our time explaining the astrology—we'll focus entirely on the cards.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

For Tarot, it doesn't matter! For Astrology, it's essential. My timing techniques (Profections, Transits) rely on a precise birth time. If you don't have it, I recommend a Tarot-only session for the most accuracy.

Philosophy & Practice

Is this "predicting the future"?

I see it more like a weather report. I look at the currents and patterns so you can decide whether to swim with them or against them. Free will is everything; the cards and stars just help you see the terrain so you can navigate it intentionally.

What topics are off-limits?

I do not provide readings on health issues, legal outcomes, or "death dates." My goal is empowerment and self-reflection; I am not a substitute for a doctor, lawyer, or therapist.

Where are you located?

I am based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. While most of my work is done via Zoom or TikTok, I am open to in-person sessions for locals—just mention it when you book!
